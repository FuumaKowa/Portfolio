About Me

Hi, I am currently a first-year Multimedia Computing Degree student at UiTM Shah Alam, and I’m proud to have already reached Semester 4 through credit exemptions. My passion lies in combining creativity with technology, as seen in my projects such as a vehicle model and animation using Blender (Semester 3) and an interactive website from my diploma studies.

I have hands-on experience from an internship at Destini Bhd., where I played a significant role as an IT Specialist. My responsibilities ranged from server maintenance and network configuration to creating innovative solutions like designing executive cards with QR codes for streamlined communication. Working closely with executives and technical teams honed my problem-solving and collaboration skills.

I’m driven by a goal to one day work at Riot Games in their multimedia department, contributing to their innovative work in animation, design, or UI/UX. In the short term, I aim to refine my skills in creating interactive and user-friendly websites. Determined and detail-oriented, I strive to give my all in every project I undertake, combining my love for art, technology, and creativity.

Outside of academics and work, I enjoy exploring new editing techniques, drawing, and reading poetry. I also have a passion for music, particularly in the metal genre, which inspires much of my creativity.
